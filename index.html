Quit for Good
by The Hodgehegs
Changing lives around the world


Our App is designed to help people from 18-60 years old get rid of an addiction, be it smoking or any major drug. The user will be given accurate statistics about how much money they have saved, how much it has benefitted different aspects of their lives and how much money they have given to a charity of their choice. Bearing in mind that they will be donating to a charity of their own decision; they will be relatively attached to it, giving the user a bigger incentive to quit the addiction. In short our app combines quitting an addiction and giving to charity hence the name “Quit For Good”.
